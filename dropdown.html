ABOUT


SIX AND A HALF is a brand dedicated to producing essential shoes for women. Distinctive designs are culminated with relentless exercises in precision and balance, in pursuit of recognizable quality and luxury. Every element is handpicked to deliver unique individuality. The matching color and texture are amalgamated by a team of professionals to manifest the personality of each shoe.

Designed to be worn beyond the season, embracing lasting quality with timeless design, attention to detail, and uncompromising lines. Every shoe is carefully made to your order.
